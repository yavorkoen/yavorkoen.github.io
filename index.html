About

I've always had an interest in computer technologies but never indulged myself in them seriously until the outburst of the Covid-19 pandemic. I got really interested in front-end web development. What hooked me to it was that one can immediately see the results of his work, represented in a visual way and also the fact that there is design involved in it. I started messing around with HTML and CSS and discovered the great possibilities for creating dynamic web content. Curiosity brought me to the JavaScript technological stack and to the SoftUni JavaScript Program which I enrolled. Since September 2020 I have been learning the ins and outs of front-end development and discovered a vast universe of knowledge to explore. What I lack is the experience in a real production environment.
